FIRST VISIT

First Visit ¬∑ What to Expect

STEP 1

Detailed Consultation & Constitution Assessment

We'll discuss your main concerns, medical history, daily routines, diet, emotions, and sources of stress. Using traditional Chinese medicine diagnostic methods (observation, listening, questioning, and pulse-taking), we'll identify your constitution type and find the root cause.

STEP 2

Personalized Treatment Plan

Based on your constitution and current condition, we'll work together to create a plan including acupuncture frequency, herbal therapy, and simple home practices such as sleep hygiene, dietary adjustments, and acupressure points.

STEP 3

Gentle, Sustainable Healing

Most conditions benefit from a treatment cycle of several weeks to months. We'll adjust the treatment pace based on your feedback and body's response, allowing improvement to happen naturally rather than forcing the body.
